Pig Dice!

Welcome to Pig Dice! Enter your names and click "Play" to play.




How the game works:

  • Goal: Get 100 points before opponent.
  • Your starting Total score is 0
  • Everytime you click the roll button a dice is rolled and the score is added to your Temporary score
  • That score is only added to your Total score if you click End Turn! button, willfully ending your turn, and now it is your opponents turn
  • If you roll a 1 then your Temporary score goes to 0 and your turn ends
